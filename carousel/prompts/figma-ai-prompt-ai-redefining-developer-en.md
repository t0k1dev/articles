## How to use this prompt

1. Open Figma and create a new file (or use the blank file
   at https://www.figma.com/design/hcCDfc69BOb7FjEkNXbsAS).
2. Open the Figma AI panel (the stars icon, top right).
3. Paste the prompt below exactly as written.
4. After generation, review each slide against the copy
   in the Source section at the bottom of this file.

---

## Prompt

Design a carousel of 8 square frames (1080x1080px each) for
Instagram and LinkedIn. The brand is t0k1dev — a technical
content brand for developers. The design should be
typography-only: no illustrations, no icons, no stock photos.
All copy should be exactly as specified below.

**Visual style**
- Background: near-black (#0D0D0D) on all slides
- Primary accent color: violet (#7C3AED)
- Body text: off-white (#E5E5E5)
- Muted text: gray (#737373), used for sources and secondary
  copy
- Contrast block background: dark navy (#1A1A2E), used only
  on Slide 7
- Font: Inter Bold for headlines, Inter Regular for body.
  If Inter is unavailable use Space Grotesk.
- 80px padding on all sides of every frame
- Every frame has "@t0k1dev" in the bottom-left corner,
  16px, gray (#737373)
- Every frame has its slide number in the top-right corner,
  16px, gray (#737373)

---

**Slide 1 — Cover**
Large centered text, two lines:
"The job title is the same."
"The job is not."
The second line "The job is not." should be in violet
(#7C3AED), Bold, larger than the first line. Nothing else
on the slide except the handle at the bottom-left.

---

**Slide 2 — The pattern**
Three lines of body text, left-aligned, stacked with
generous line height:
"npm felt like cheating. Then everyone had it."
"Cloud felt like cheating. Then everyone had it."
"AI feels like cheating. You know where this goes."
The third line should be Bold or in violet to give it
slightly more visual weight than the first two.

---

**Slide 3 — The uncomfortable truth**
Large dominant stat at the top: "70%" in violet (#7C3AED),
very large (120px+).
Below it, a subheading in off-white:
"of developer time goes to maintenance, debugging, and
technical debt — not building new things."
Below that, smaller body text in gray:
"Most of the code we wrote was assembly of known parts.
AI just does that faster now."
At the bottom, source text in gray 16px:
"Stripe, survey of 11,000+ developers"

---

**Slide 4 — The bottleneck moved**
Top: large bold text in off-white:
"Copilot users finish tasks 55% faster."
"55% faster" should be in violet.
Middle: body text:
"But writing code was already a minority of the workday."
Bottom: two-line punchline in violet, bold:
"AI didn't speed up the work.
It showed which part was always the hard part."
Source in gray 16px: "GitHub Copilot study, 2022"

---

**Slide 5 — What changed**
Four short lines stacked, left-aligned, body text size,
off-white, regular weight:
"You write less code."
"You make more decisions."
"You review output you didn't write."
"You define constraints before anything exists."
A horizontal divider below the list.
Below the divider, one bold line in violet, larger:
"The job didn't disappear. It moved upstream."

---

**Slide 6 — One dev outships a team**
Body text, left-aligned, off-white, regular weight:
"A developer I know shipped a full SaaS in 6 weeks. Alone.
Auth, billing, dashboard, background jobs, API."
Second paragraph, same size:
"Not a prodigy. Just full context and no alignment meetings."
Bold closing line in violet:
"46% of code in Copilot-enabled files is now AI-generated."
Source in gray 16px: "GitHub Octoverse, 2023"

---

**Slide 7 — Skills**
Split the slide into two vertical blocks with a dividing
line or clear visual separation.

Left block on dark navy background (#1A1A2E):
Label in violet, uppercase, 18px: "GAINING VALUE"
Three lines in off-white, 26px, each prefixed with —:
"— System design"
"— Critical code review"
"— Writing precise problem descriptions"

Right block on default dark background (#0D0D0D):
Label in gray, uppercase, 18px: "LOSING VALUE"
Three lines in gray, 26px, reduced opacity, each prefixed
with —:
"— Memorizing syntax"
"— Writing boilerplate"
"— Configuring standard tooling"

---

**Slide 8 — CTA**
Body text, left-aligned, off-white, regular weight, 28px:
"Pick one real project.
Use AI on it every day for a month.
Not to generate everything —
to learn when to trust it and when to throw it away."
Bold violet line below, separated by spacing:
"The shift already happened. Work with it deliberately."
Footer text in gray, 18px, bottom of the slide:
"tokidev.com · @t0k1dev"
